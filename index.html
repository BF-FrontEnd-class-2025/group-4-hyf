THE PROGRAM

Training people in IT significantly boosts their chances for a fulfilling, long-term employment. Simultaneously, we help meeting the demand for tech talent on the job market.

We teach full-stack web-development, with a focus on modern Javascript. Trainees learn to work in a modern tech team and to become well-rounded and proactive professionals.

Companies hire our graduates because of their drive, attitude and learning agility. Six years ago, HackYourFuture was founded to tackle the challenging un(der)employment of people with a refugee background, while addressing the ever-growing demand for tech talent. We have helped over 300 of our graduates in the Netherlands, and over 600 worldwide, into meaningful, high-quality jobs.
